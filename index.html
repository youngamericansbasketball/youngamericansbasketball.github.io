Affordable World Class Coaching

Welcome to Young Americans Basketball Club, the #1 club in Southern Arizona for developing great players. With over 30 years of professional experience, we specialize in player development guranteed to take every player's game to the next level. We work with players of all skill levels and ages, so whether you're looking to join a team to enjoy casual play in our development leagues, try out for our competative club teams to compete in the nations most prestigious tournaments, or just want to sharpen your skills with Coach Joe's training sessions, we have something for every player.

High School Development League



The Young American High School Development League is perfect for players looking to have a great time while working on their basketball skills and fundamentals. Our 5-on-5 format includes referees and score keeping to simulate a high school game environment. Your league fee includes 7 practices, 6 games, and program that is designed to get you ready for high school basketball.

Now accepting registrations and getting started is easy! Just click the link below to sign up and show up to your Placement Event at 3:15 PM on Saturday, January 13th, at Tanque Verde Lutheran Church.

Our Coaches

Coach Joe

With over 30 years of experience, you will find no better coach in Souther Arizona. Coach Joe has experience at all levels of play and has helped hundreds of players achieve their goal of playing college basketball.

Coach James

Before coming to Young Americans, James played basketball for Saguaro High School in Tucson, AZ. After having a couple young boys, he began coaching them and had success as the Head Coach of their YMCA team. While still highly competative, James is passionate about teaching kids and molding young minds.

Coach Nikki

After enjoying a successful high school basketball career, Nikki earned a BS degree from the University of Arizona. In addition to her 25+ years of professional fitness training, her passion for the game of basketball is almost as strong as her passion for seeing her players grow and develop.

Coach Ricky

After playing basketball his whole life, Ricky began coaching backetball in 2015. He is a passionate coach who has led many championship winning teams including Arizona Basketball Events and West Coast Elite titles. His mark of success is seeing his players fall in love with the game while using it's many lessons to become the best version of themselves off the court.
