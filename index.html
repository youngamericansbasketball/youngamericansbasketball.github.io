Affordable World Class Coaching

Welcome to Young Americans Basketball Club, the #1 club in Southern Arizona for developing great players. With over 30 years of professional experience, we specialize in player development guranteed to take every player's game to the next level. We work with players of all skill levels and ages, so whether you're looking to join a team to enjoy casual play in our development leagues, try out for our competative club teams to compete in the nations most prestigious tournaments, or just want to sharpen your skills with Coach Joe's training sessions, we have something for every player.

Development League



The Young American Development League is perfect for newer players looking to have a great time while working on their basketball skills and fundementals. Our 4-on-4 format, small rosters and professional coaching guarantees that your player will greatly develop their skills with lots of faced-paced game experience. Your league fee includes 7 practices, 6 games with runner-up and championship medals handed out at the end of the season.


Competative Club Basketball


Young American Club Teams are for those players looking to compete with against the best. The goal for all of our club players is simple - develop their skills and expose their talent to prepare them for high school basketball and beyond. We will be competing in some of the nations best tournaments against some of the premier and most prestigeous clubs.

  • Cost: $80/month
  • Practice Dates: Every Monday and Thursday
  • Practice Times: 5-6 PM / 6-7:30 PM / 7:30-9 PM (depending on age)
  • Practice Location: Tanque Verde Lutheran Church / Christ Presbyterian Church
  • Ages: 1st Grade - High School

Training



Do you just want to take your game to the next level with the best pleyer development program in Southern Arizona? You've come to the right place. Coach Joe offers individual and group training sessions guaranteed to produce results almost instantly. Coach Joe's high-level training develops essential skills such as ball handling, footwork and shooting. Starting at just $20/session, we guarantee you will be sorry you didn't start sooner.
