COVID-19 Policies
Young Americans Basketball Club takes the COVID-19 pandemic very seriously and we are committed to protecting the health and safety of our players and staff by ensuring that we adhere to guidelines set by the CDC and Arizona Department of Health Services. We strongly ask any player or staff member who has any COVID-19 related symptoms to stay home and consult with a medical professional before returning to the club. Thank you and please don't hesitate to let us know if you have any questions or concerns.
