Young American Club Teams
Young American Club Teams are for those players looking to compete with against the best. The goal for all of our club players is simple - develop their skills and expose their talent to prepare them for high school basketball an beyond. We will be competing in some of the nations best tournaments against some of the premier and most prestigeous clubs. Here are some of the details:
  • Cost: $80/month
  • Practice Dates: Every Monday and Thursday
  • Practice Times: 6-7 PM / 7-8 PM / 8-9 PM (depending on age)
  • Practice Location: Christ Presbyterian Church
  • Ages: 1st Grade - High School
