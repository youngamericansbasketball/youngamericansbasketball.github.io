Tucson Basketball Factory Development League
The Young American Development League is perfect for newer players or players just looking to have a great time while working on their basketball skills and fundementals. What separates us from all of the other leagues and clubs in Southern Arizona is that your player will get professional coaching guaranteed develop your game. Signing up is easy, just select the Register for League button below, submit the form, and Coach Joe will be in touch soon to get you started.
  • Cost: $80
  • Practice Days: Every Friday
  • Practice Times: 5 PM (K - 2nd Grade) & 6 PM (3rd - 6th Grade)
  • Practice Location: Tanque Verde Lutheran Church
  • Ages: K - High School
  • # of Practices: 7
  • # of Games: 7
  • Start Date: June 19th, 2021
  • End Date: August 14th, 2021
