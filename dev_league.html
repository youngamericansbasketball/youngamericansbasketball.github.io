Tucson Basketball Factory Development League
The Young American Development League is perfect for newer players or players just looking to have a great time while working on their basketball skills and fundementals. What separates us from all of the other leagues and clubs in Southern Arizona is that your player will get professional coaching guaranteed develop your game.
Details:
  • Cost: $90 (plus a $15 jersey fee if this is your first time with the league)
  • Practice Days: Every Friday
  • Practice Times: 5 PM (K - 2nd Grade) & 6 PM (3rd - 6th Grade)
  • Practice Location: Tanque Verde Lutheran Church
  • Ages: Pre School - 6th Grade
  • # of Practices: 7
  • # of Games: 6
  • Start Date: September 4th, 2021
  • End Date: October 30th, 2021
Signing up is easy, just follow these steps:
  • Step 1: Select the Register for League button below and submit the form
  • Step 2: Show up to the next practice
  • Step 3: Bring payment to the placement event (cash, check or Venmo accepted)
  • Step 4: Reach out to Coach Joe (520.347.0709) with any questions
