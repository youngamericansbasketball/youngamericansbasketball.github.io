Tucson Basketball Factory Girls Development League
The Young American Development Girls League is perfect for newer players or players just looking to have a great time while working on their basketball skills and fundementals. What separates us from all of the other leagues and clubs in Southern Arizona is that your player will get professional coaching guaranteed develop your game.
Details:
  • Cost: $90 (plus a $15 jersey fee for new players)
  • Practice Days: Fridays
  • Practice Times: 5 PM (K - 2nd Grade) & 6 PM (3rd - 6th Grade)
  • Game Days: Saturdays
  • Game Times: TBD

  • Ages: Pre School - 6th Grade
  • # of Practices: 7
  • # of Games: 6
  • Start Date: November 6th, 2021
  • End Date: December 18th, 2021

    • Pre-K / Kindergarten: 8 AM

    • 5th / 6th Grade: 12:30 PM
  • Step 3: Bring payment to the placement event (cash, check or Venmo accepted)
  • Step 4: Reach out to Coach Joe (520.347.0709) with any questions
